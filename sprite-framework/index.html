Street Walker 3

Controls:

  • Cop:
    • F: move left
    • H: move right
    • Click: move right
  • Guy:
    • J: move left
    • L: move right
    • Click: move left

Basic Example

Controls:

  • W: move up
  • S: move down
  • A: move left
  • D: move right

Note: You must click on (select) the ball before you can move it with the arrow keys. You can also move the cop and guy in the background above if they are selected.
